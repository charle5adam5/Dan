GALAXY HEAD

by Constant Swimmer

This album represents a time where I was experiencing a spiritual awakening. After my meditation and frequency exploration, I would set up a session and just write what came out of me. Most of these songs are a result of the frequencies I was tuned to during this period. These songs were written between 2012-2016. From beginning to end, its a journey through what you would call a Galaxy Head of sound and movement blending different styles of electronic and heavy music together. Take a trip through The "Galaxy Head" Peace and Love All songs written, recorded, mixed and mastered by Daniel Curtis Hughes of DarthSound Productions Keyboards, programming, FX's - Daniel Hughes Drums, percussion, programming - Daniel Hughes Guitar on track 3 performed by Felipe Sanchez Guitar on track 2 performed and written by Kyle Hoffer Saxophone on track 11 performed and written by Corey Peterson All Rights Reserved 2016. @Constant Swimmer @DatthSound Productions CREDITS RELEASED MAY 13, 2016
